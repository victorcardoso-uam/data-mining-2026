# Summary: Decision Tree Structure for Streaming Retention
**Team:** Team-06

## Key Concepts
Decision tree structures help classify data and predict outcomes by breaking down complex datasets into a series of logical, attribute-based choices. They provide a visual map of decisions and their possible consequences, making it easier to interpret models for technical applications.

## Project Application: Reducing Churn Rate
In this project, we use decision trees to analyze user behavior and preferences to prevent subscription cancellations (churn). By mapping out user attributes—such as watch history, genre preferences, and viewing frequency—the model helps the platform recommend content that matches the customer's tastes. This logical structure ensures users find relevant content quickly, increasing engagement and reducing the likelihood of them canceling the service due to a lack of interesting options.
