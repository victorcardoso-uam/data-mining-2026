#In this energy optimization project, a decision tree algorithm could be used to identify the main factors that affect the performance of photovoltaic (PV) systems. In this case, it could analyze variables such as energy generation levels, solar irradiance, panel temperature, cleaning frequency, inverter alarms, and weather conditions to determine why a system is not reaching its expected generation target.

By using historical operational data, the decision tree could help categorize system performance (for example: normal performance vs. underperformance) and identify the most important factors causing efficiency losses, this would support better maintenance decisions, such as prioritizing panel cleaning, inspecting specific components, or adjusting maintenance schedules. As a result, the company could improve energy production, reduce unnecessary maintenance activities, and make data-driven operational decisions.#
